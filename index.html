Check out if your birthday is Palindrome.

A palindrome is a number/word that remains the same when its digits/letter's are reversed.

Let's Go

Enter your birthdate & we will tell you if your birthdate is a palindrome

This app checks your birthdate in 3 formats yyyy-mm-dd, mm-dd-yyyy and mm-dd-yy

e.g. if your birthdate is 16 May 1998, then app will check for 19980516, 05161998, 051698
